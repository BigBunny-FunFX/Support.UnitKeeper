Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. UnitKeeper does not upload your information to any server. Your properties, tenants, photos, and financial records stay on your phone.

What happens if I delete the app?

If you delete UnitKeeper, your data will be removed from your device. We recommend keeping regular backups of your device through iCloud or your computer.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

The free version includes full functionality for up to 2 properties. UnitKeeper Pro ($6.99, one-time) unlocks unlimited properties. There are no feature restrictions, time limits, or subscriptions.

Can I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Yes. Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchase." Your Pro upgrade is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same account.

How do I add a photo?

When adding or viewing a property, tenant, maintenance issue, or expense, look for the "Add Photo" button. You can take a new photo with your camera or choose one from your photo library.

Will cloud sync be added?

We're exploring cloud sync and backup options for a future update. For now, your device's built-in iCloud backup will preserve your data.

What devices are supported?

UnitKeeper requires an iPhone running iOS 17 or later.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@unitkeeper.app. Include your device model and iOS version if you're reporting a technical issue.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

UnitKeeper ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a priority. The App stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

Data Collection

UnitKeeper does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect analytics, usage data, or device information.
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any tracking technologies.
  • We do not collect location data.

Data Storage

All information you enter into UnitKeeper — including property details, tenant information, maintenance records, financial data, contacts, and photos — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local data storage frameworks. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Photos and Camera

UnitKeeper requests access to your device's camera and photo library so you can attach photos to properties, tenants, maintenance issues, and expenses. These photos are stored locally on your device within the app's data container. They are not uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

In-App Purchases

UnitKeeper offers an optional in-app purchase (UnitKeeper Pro) through Apple's App Store. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the purchase transaction. You can review Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Third-Party Services

UnitKeeper does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The App does not contain ads.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to share.

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete individual records within the app, or delete all data by uninstalling UnitKeeper from your device.

Children's Privacy

UnitKeeper is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or UnitKeeper's data practices, please contact us at support@unitkeeper.app.
